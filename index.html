Welcome to a new way of life.

I'm Jenelle, a 22 year old adventurer, photographer, web developer-in-training, and introvert.

Until this year, I lived my entire life in the suburbs of Colorado. This year, I left everything I knew about that comfortable lifestyle and headed out to Southeast Asia to begin a new adventure.

This blog is to document those adventures and my new life, and more importantly to share what I discover with others. On Noble Vita, you'll find my musings about careers, travel, relationships, lifestyle, and pretty much everything in between.

I believe in living life in a way that allows for independence, adventure, autonomy and freedom. Freedom to do what you want, go where you want, make what you want, and live how you want.

The only limits in life are the ones we impose on ourselves. Join me in the journey to smash those limitations and create your life - your way! :)
